<h3> Voltage Divider Sensors </h3> <img src='../02_cutting/electronics/VoltageDivider.png' alt='Voltage divider'> <br> <p> Recall the concept of a voltage divider. You can replace a fixed-value resistor with an analog sensor like a thermistor or Hall sensor. Changing the value of the fixed resistor is a way to tune the output to a particular range. </p> <h3> Potentiometers </h3> <img src='../02_cutting/electronics/Potentiometer.png'> <br> <p> A potentiometer is a voltage divider with a movable contact.
