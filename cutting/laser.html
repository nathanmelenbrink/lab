### [Thunder Laser Nova24](https://thunderlaserusa.com/) in SC 103 ### Standard Operating Procedure - Turn on Exhaust fan - Turn on Cooling water - Turn on both laser switches - Open LightBurn <img width="75%" src="./LightBurnBaseScreen.PNG" alt="LightBurn"> - Import vector files or bitmaps. <img width="75%" src="./LightburnImportedFile.PNG" alt="ImportedImage"> - Insert material - On the laser control panel - Hit esc (if necessary) and move head to origin - Hit ORIGIN to set origin - Adjust laser height (use Z/U button and side arrows). Check with 6 mm spacer. - Back on the computer, set power and speed. <img width="35%" src="./LightBurnSettings.PNG" alt="LightburnSettings"> - Hit “Start” ### Shutdown Procedure - Turn off both laser switches. - Turn off Cooling water. - Turn off Exhaust fan. ### Safety issues - Always keep within the tape on the floor in fron of laser while cutting. Watch during the entire cut. - Wait a few minutes after cutting plastics before opening the laser cutter to allow for outgassing. - If a fire occurs, hit the emergency stop, turn off exhaust fan and wait for fire to subside. - If it does not subside, use your judgement to fight fire with fire blanket or [extinguisher](https://www.osha.gov/etools/evacuation-plans-procedures/emergency-standards/portable-extinguishers/use). - When fire is beyond safe control, Call operations on the red phone to report fire. Pull fire alarm. Exit building. ### Cutting - lines (vector files) <br> - kerf <br> <img width="400" src="./kerf2.png" alt="kerf"> <img width="400" src="./kerf1.png" alt="kerf"> - speed and power - <a href='./engraving.html'> images (raster files)</a> ### Materials - Most anything plant-derived: cardboard, wood, leather, fiber... - Some plastics: Acrylic (plexiglass), Delrin - Never cut: PVC plastic, ABS plastic.
